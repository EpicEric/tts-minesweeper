Instructions

  1. Open Tabletop Simulator first. This is important: if you run the game after downloading, it'll automatically delete the files.
  2. Preferably from Mozilla Firefox or Google Chrome, open this page, choose your options (either difficulty or custom settings), and click "Generate and Save".
  3. After downloading the Minesweeper.json file, find your Tabletop Simulator save folder. It's usually one of the following:
    • Windows: C:\Users\USERNAME\Documents\My Games\Tabletop Simulator\Saves
    • OS X: ~/My Games/Tabletop Simulator/Saves
  4. Move your generated file to the folder above as TS_Save_X.json, replacing X with the next number in the count accordingly.
